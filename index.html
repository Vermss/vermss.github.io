Who We Are

The Laurier Ski and Snowboard Club is run by students for students. We organize trips and events to help students connect with other skiers and boarders as well as shred some of that fresh gnar.

Travel

Travel to various ski & snowboard resort destinations in Canada and the US.

Meet People

Participate in our events and trips to meet like-minded skiing and riding enthusiasts.

Stay Active

The club is a great way to stay active during those cold winter months.

Events

A breakdown of the trips and events we will be running throughout the year

Info Night

Come out and meet some of the other member of the club while seeing what we have planned for the year.

Movie Night in the Pool

A super fun night where we set up a projector on the deck and everyone jumps into inner tubes and hangs out watching a movie.

Sky Zone

A way to de-stress during midterms by jumping on trampolines and forgetting your problems.

Pub Crawl

A great way to get to know members off the slopes where we hop from bar to bar and have an awesome time.

LSSC Olympics

A night of games and fun where teams compete for prizes and to become LSSC Olympians.

Mont Sutton

The LSSC's marquee trip that is 3 days and 3 nights in a giant 36 person chalet just south of Montreal at Mont Sutton.
